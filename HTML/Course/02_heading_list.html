Block Level Elements

These are the elements that take full width from Left to Right.

  • Headings h1 to h6
  • Paragraphs p
  • Lists ul, ol
  • List Items li
  • And many more...

Inline Elements

These are the elements that take full width from Left to Rihgt.

  • Image Tag i
  • Hyperlink Tag a
  • Span Tag s
  • And many more...

Div Tag?

Oh it's just a wrapper that's block level by default.
